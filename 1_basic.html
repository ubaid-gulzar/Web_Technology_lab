TOURIST SPOTS OF KASHMIR

PAHALGAM

Pahalgam, a picturesque town in Kashmir, is known for its lush green valleys, snow-capped mountains, and serene rivers. It serves as a base for the Amarnath Yatra and offers activities like trekking, horse riding, and river rafting. Its natural beauty makes it a popular destination for tourists.

SONMARG

Sonmarg, meaning 'Meadow of Gold', is a beautiful valley surrounded by glaciers and alpine meadows. It is a popular spot for trekking, camping, and starting point for the Zoji La Pass. The scenic landscapes and fresh mountain air make it a must-visit destination in Kashmir.
